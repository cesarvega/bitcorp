Brand Institute is the global leader in brand name and identity development. We partner with clients across industries and develop some of the world’s most iconic brands.

We specialize in pharmaceutical/healthcare-related branding and name over 75% of drugs approved worldwide. Our wholly owned regulatory subsidiary, Drug Safety Institute (DSI), is comprised of full-time experts who formerly served as key naming, packaging and labeling regulatory officials at FDA, EMA, Health Canada, AMA and WHO. In their former roles, many of our regulatory experts authored the name safety guidance currently in use at their respective agencies. Add in the industry’s most prolific creative teams, a group of seasoned trademark attorneys and paralegals, a proven and proprietary research model, 14 consecutive years with a majority global Share of Market Regulatory Name Approvals, and you have a recipe for success. We thank all of our clients for making us #1 in pharmaceutical and healthcare naming!

Brand Institute is proud to have partnered on the products!
